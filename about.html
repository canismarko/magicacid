About the Magic Acid Blog

What's the Blog About?

Short answer: Mostly chemistry but whatever I feel like at the time

Long answer: I will mostly focus on chemistry in a very general sense. Before starting grad school I worked for a spice extraction company so food science topics will probably show up from time to time, especially concerning the dialog between natural versus artificial since that came up a lot while I worked there. I focused on antioxidants which is an often abused term so I may write a little about the chemistry going on there.

Here's a (not exclusive) list:

  • Energy and chemistry in society
  • Everyday things that use chemistry
  • Ingredients and food (especially natural vs synthetic)
  • Atheism, religion and secularism
  • Computer programming, especially open source
  • Open science and data exchange
  • Interplay between politics and science

Who are you?

Just a guy who loves science and geeky things. I'm currently working on my PhD in physical chemistry at the University of Illinois at Chicago. My research focuses on the characterization of electrochemical reactions in lithium-ion batteries.I also have a bit of a fascination with computers so the combination of using computers to do science is particularly interesting to me.

Outside of a professional setting, I enjoy playing soccer, snowboarding, music, and dungeons and dragons. I play bass although not that well. If I can afford it, I'll gladly go anywhere anywhen and hit the mountain. My most zen moments all involve fresh powder.
